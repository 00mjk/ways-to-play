Fox & Geese
Draft

#Terminology

In other languages and countries it is known as:

  • Icelandic: refskák, ‘fox chess’.a[p. 146]
  • French: jeu de renard, ‘game of the fox’.a[p. 148]
  • German: der fuchs und die gänse ‘fox and geese’, or fush- und hühnerspiel ‘fox and hens game’, or fuchs im hühnerhof ‘fox in the chicken yard’.a[p. [146, 152]]
  • Italian: lupo e pecore, ‘wolf and sheep’, or giuoco della volpe ‘game of the fox’.a[p. 148]
